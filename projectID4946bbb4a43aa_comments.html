In P2 please limit yourself to 15-20 pages per Qual. project.

The Santals are a people in Central India, speaking their own Austro-Asiatic language (Santali), quite different from the surrounding Indo-European languages (Hindi, Bengali, Oriya). Their unique culture has been a subject of study, and already PG includes one book on them, Folklore of the Santal Parganas by Cecil Henry Bompas. This book is again an welcome addition to the available materials in our collection.
